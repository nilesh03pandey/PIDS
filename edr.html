The Evolving Threat Landscape

Traditional antivirus relies on signatures—knowing what a "bad file" looks like. However, our research shows that 58% of modern attacks involves no malware files at all ("Living off the Land").

Attackers are now logging in, not breaking in. EDRStart Research has identified a critical shift towards identity-based attacks and script-based execution that bypasses legacy controls.

Key Research Finding

"The gap between attacker sophistication and defender visibility is widening. Organizations relying solely on prevention controls miss 7 out of 10 lateral movement attempts."

Phase 1: Assess & Inventory

Before purchasing tools, organizations must understand what they are protecting. The Assessment phase involves mapping the digital terrain. Blind spots in asset inventory are the primary cause of failed EDR deployments.

Critical Actions:

  • ✓ Conduct a full endpoint inventory (Workstations, Servers, BYOD).
  • ✓ Identify legacy OS versions incompatible with modern agents.
  • ✓ Define data privacy requirements for telemetry collection.
